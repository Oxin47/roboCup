Pointsgame League 2013

On april 28 starts a new *yearly* points tournament Pointsgame League 2013 !
Ideologically, we consider the league as a continuation of RoboCups, so-called "blitz-CHAOS" and manual tournaments.
So, what is our PL 2013 ?
  1. First of all, it's a major tournament consisting of one-day RoboCups.
    To take a part on any of these RoboCups you should just be at zagram and enter "!register" into the public chat, as always.
  2. Each tournament is Swiss-based. Each won game gives you 4 points, each draw gives 2, each loss gives 1.
    Tournament results are summed up -- that is exactly the final league result table.
  3. Only the best 15 tournament results are counted into the final sum.
    Overall number of appointments is 18. This corresponds to about "once in 2 weeks".
  4. We ask to take a part in the tournament only if you plan to play at least half of the games.
    To prevent cheating a pair of (slightly messy) rules are applied:
    • "Dummy" players, e.g. those who played less than half of the games, get 0 points in any case.
    • After the tournament we count the number of non-dummy players, and overall number of participants. If these tho numbers lie in different "powers of two", e.g. if one additional round started because of the "dummy" players, players get a 4 point less value into the final league sum.
      If there was no "power of two" switch, then simple number of gained points is counted in.
  5. Everyone can take a part in each of the tournaments.
    But, if one wants his results to be summed up, he must always play using the same nickname. Also, tournament organizers may ask to confirm that he's not a "clone" of some other player.
  6. We plan to variate tournament rules a lot. We plan to include tournaments based on polish rules.:) The same with 4-cross, blitz and not blitz etc.
  7. In the spirit of official rules we note, as always, that organizers preserve the right to change tournament rules if they deem it necessary.
