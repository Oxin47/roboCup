Gry

Specific rules

Individual rules of this tournament:

  • Tournament organization scheme is "King of the hill", which is also a variation of the swiss tournament system.
  • All players play an equal number of rounds.
  • Number of rounds depends on number of participants:
    5-8 players 9-16 players 17-32 players 33-64 players
    5 rounds 6 rounds 7 rounds 8 rounds
  • The goal of a player is to maximize points taken in the tournament. Each win gives 4 points, each draw gives 2 points, each loss gives 1 point.
  • At start of each round all players are sorted by obtained points. The strongest player is matched in a game with the second. The third with the fourth. And so on.
    Example. Suppose players had 17, 15, 14, 13, 13, 11 points. Then assigned games will be:
    "17"-"15",
    "14"-"some of the 13",
    "the other 13"-"11".
  • Czas pomiędzy rundami (na odpoczynek) jest ustawiony na minut.
  • Ustawienia gry: 3 minuty na pierwszy ruch, 1 minuta na rozpoczęcie, s/ruch, gra towarzyska, pozycja początkowa 1 X.
